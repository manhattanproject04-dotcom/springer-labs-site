Common questions

What to Expect

What does a retainer engagement look like?
Retainers are structured around your specific goals, not a fixed package. We begin with a deep diagnostic of your operations, identify where AI and strategy create the most leverage, then build and implement alongside your team. Engagements typically run 3–6 months minimum.
Why retainer-only? Why not project-based?
Real strategic change doesn't happen in a single deliverable. Retainer engagements allow us to stay in the room, adapt as conditions change, and ensure the systems we build actually stick. We only take seven clients at a time to protect the quality of that attention.
What kinds of organizations do you work with?
We work with clean energy developers, nonprofits and workforce development organizations, and media companies. The common thread: mission-driven leadership that wants to build lasting impact, not just a strategy document.
How do I know if Springer Labs is the right fit?
If you're serious about implementing AI infrastructure (not just exploring it) and you're building something with real-world stakes, we're probably a fit. The best first step is a conversation. Reach out and we'll tell you honestly whether we can help.
What does the onboarding process look like?
After an initial call, we conduct a structured operational audit, reviewing your current workflows, team capacity, and strategic goals. From there, we propose a phased engagement plan before any commitment is made.
